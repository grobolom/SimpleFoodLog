How does it work?

Please enter the food, followed by the number of calories, as follows:

A delicious choripan sandwich 250

you may also put 'calories' or 'kcal' or 'c' or 'cal' at the end - the log will handle all of it. Once you've typed out your food and hit enter, it will get saved to your log and get tracked for the next rolling week.

Why did I build it?

Food logs are too complex. I don't need all the fancy recipe lookups and repeated meals. I just want to put in the calories and walk away. Here's the result.
